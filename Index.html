About this application

This private application has been developed exclusively for the ministry El León de Judá to automate the publication of official video content on the organization's TikTok account using the official TikTok Content Posting API.

✔ No Third-Party Accounts

Purpose

The application publishes sermon clips, Bible teachings, Christian devotionals, and other official content created by El León de Judá Church. The application is intended solely for internal organizational use.
